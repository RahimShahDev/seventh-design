About Us

Boxy is the very first smart mailbox to launch in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We've set out to revolutionise e-commerce and deliveries in KSA and the whole of the Arab World, making life easier for customers and for delivery agents and ecommerce businesses.

We make it easier to send and receive, return, and even pay by cash on delivery.

Our Values

Here at Boxy, we have a dedicated team that works to a system of rules and values to make life easier for everyone involved. Our customers love us for our values, which include:

  • Always working hard in order to provide the best service to our customers and other partners.
